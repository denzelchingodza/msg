# Your championship photos go here

Drop `.jpg` / `.png` / `.webp` files in this folder and they automatically
appear in the Championship '26 gallery, after the story cards.

The filename becomes the caption: `game4_og_tipin.jpg` → "GAME4 OG TIPIN".

This folder is gitignored — NBA/Getty photos are copyrighted, so keep them
local. Your own parade photos are yours; commit at your discretion.
